Project Overview¶

Our project mainly focuses on the daily trending videos on YouTube and hopefully the analysis of which will reveal an insight of what is common between the popular videos and the topics appealing to different users. Here are the questions we are interested in:

  1. What are the common trends on YouTube in general? What are the top 10 videos that have received large number user appreciation in a particular year/month? What are the top videos in different countries in a particular time?

  2. What factors affect the popularity of a YouTube video? Which categories of YouTube videos are the most popular? Which video category (e.g. entertainment, gaming, comedy, etc.) has the largest number of trending videos? What are the most common words in trending video titles? What are the top 10 most popular tags? What types of tags frequently appear in the most top-trending videos? Which YouTube channels have the largest number of trending videos? Is posting time a factor affect the popularity of a YouTube video? (if yes, then we continue to answer the next question)

  3. What is the best posting time on Youtube to be on the top trending? When were the trending videos usually published? On which days of the week? At which times of the day?

From the basic data procession that we have performed so far, we gain a basic understanding of the dataset. In general, we found out that there were certain videos trending in different days.
Besides, many of the trending videos shared similar texts in their titles, some of which can reveal a glimpse of the common characteristics of the trending videos.

For the following parts of our project, we will focus on the specific answers of the questions above through data visualization tools and give out insights behind the answers. Each one is in separate command box.

Milestones and Progress¶

We completed data preprocessing and did some of data analysis. Belows are what we have done in data preprocessing part:

1. Data collection¶

We use the dataset on Kaggle.com: Trending Youtube video statistics. There are total 9 datasets, each dataset has information of trending videos in a different country. We merge all the data at the end of the notebook and will arrange it in future steps.

2. Indexing, selection, and filtering¶

  • We filter and remove all duplicate records, or records that have all null values.
  • We check and preprocess the video_id to make sure all videos ID are unique.

3. Managing missing data¶

  • There are almost no missing values in this dataset, so we do not implement missing data treatment.

4. Data transformation¶

  • For trending_date column: Correct positions of month and day of trending_data column; convert the string type of trending_date column into the datetime type; format the trending_date column with yyyy-mm-dd.

  • For publish_time column: Split the publish_time into publish_date and publish_time columns; convert the string type of publish_date and publish_time columns into the datetime type.

  • Translate comment_disabled, ratings_disabled and video_error_or_removed columns from boolean values into numeric values.

  • Apply log-transformation to reduce skewness on the views column and save the result in the new log_views column.

  • Extract the weekday and weekend from the date column.

5. Text processing¶

  • We create new columns with music genres, trending topics, videos types, etc.
  • We extract the URL and text separately from the description column.
  • From the title column, we find and analyze the most common words in trending videos title.

6. Merging and reshaping data¶

  • After the data transformation and text processing process, we add newly created columns and remove unnecessary ones.

5. Normalization of data¶

Oftentime, when we are seeking for relations between variables, we tend to normalize them, which refers to bringing them down to a scale which is comparable between variables. This helps us reduce the skewness present withing the variable data, which is the one-sided majority accumulation of the variable data when a histogram of the same is plotted.
